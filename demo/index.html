Bootstrap Demo

This is a small demo site to show case the awesomeness of twitter bootstrap. This is just to show case the bootstrap capabilities such as navbar, carousel, jumbotron, forms, thumbnails, grid system and scrollspy. The photos in the site were taken by me :)
